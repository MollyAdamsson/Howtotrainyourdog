About Us

We are here to help!

Our main goal is to improve your life. We are a qualified team of four who are famous for our pioneering work in how dogs communicate, and we have identified over 30 ‘calming signals’ that have since helped many dog trainers, behaviourists and ethologists understand dog language. We offer a package deal where you get 10 lessons and we meet up once a week. You can choose from obedience training or focus training, or why not both?

We have carried out extensive research in the field of companion animal welfare, including subjects relating to responsible pet ownership, and psychoneuroimmunology, a branch of veterinary science that deals with the influence of stress and other emotional states on the nervous and immune systems. We are constantly updating our knowledge by attending in-house training and external courses, including Specialising in Dog Aggression and Sensory Integration.
